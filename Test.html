Song Announcer    


Song Announcer is a unique ios Music Player that announces (speaks) song title, artist name, and album title prior to playing a song.


Do you have a large local user music library on your iPhone?
Do you use a sound dock?
Do you listen with Bluetooth head gear?
Do you connect your iPhone to an older basic auto sound system without an lcd display?
Have you ever had a song start and you can't remember the artist or title and your phone isn't easily accessible?
Song Announcer is for you!

Music Selection:

  • playlist
  • artist
  • album
  • song
  • genre
  • composers
  • compilation

Supports:
  • Bluetooth output
  • Phone interruptions
Configuration Options:
  • speak Artist name
  • speak Album title
  • speak Song title
  • shuffle songs
  • repeat a song
  • change voice accent   (U.S., British, Australian)
Full Disclosure:
  • Local user music library only.
  • iPhone support only
  • ios 11.0 thru 12.2
  • No internationalization.   (English text and voice)
  • No ads.
  • Free LITE version.   (speaks only album title)
  • In-App purchase to upgrade to PRO version.   (enables all Configuration Options listed above)
Song Announcer is available for free in the Apple App Store.

Please give it a try.
